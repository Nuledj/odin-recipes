Potato Salad

  • 3 cups mayonnaise
  • 2 cups finely chopped pickles
  • 5 large hard-cooked eggs,chopped
  • half cup chopped red onion
  • half cup chopped celery
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • half teaspoon black pepper
